A little bit about myself.

I am Anna, a certified UI|UX designer with a background in industrial design and a specialisation in online marketing. I have more than 12 years experience in design and love to turn simple ideas into great designs that are simple, enjoyable and harmonious.

Following the Design Thinking method, I conduct competitive analyses, create user flows and wireframes. With a special passion for visual design I create mockups, style guides and the final UI design for native and responsive web applications. I always develop ideas from the user's perspective to solve their problems, because it makes me happy when users move smoothly through interfaces and find their goal in a playful way.

To keep my knowledge of UI technologies up to date, I read professional articles as well as blogs, watch tutorials and attend events. I am an out-of-the-box thinker and like to be involved in all design processes, from ideation to development and implementation.

I would like to work in a creative and innovative team where we can learn from each other. Together I would like to develop interfaces that make the user's life easier without having to think too much about it.

I love to deepen my skills in UI|UX design and to become an expert in this field. I am available for contract work in the Hamburg area or remotely.

When I am not saving the world by realizing great projects, I like to do sports like running, boxing and Pilates. I also love to cook or practice drums.
