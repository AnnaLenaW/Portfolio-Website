About Me

I am Anna, a UI|UX designer from Hamburg, Germany with a diploma in industrial design and a specialisation in online marketing. It makes me happy when users move through interfaces smoothly and find their goal playfully. I realised early on in my studies that I have a passion for 2- dimensional design. During my studies and my time as a graphic designer I learned all the skills in colour, typography and iconography. I also taught myself the most important tools to turn simple ideas into great designs that are simple, enjoyable and harmonious. For me, it was the next logical step to deepen my knowledge in UI design. I aim to work in a creative and innovative team to develop interfaces that make the user's life easier without thinking too much about it. I am currently available for both freelance and contract work in the Hamburg area or remotely. In addition to my work and passions in UI design, I also love travelling around the world and sports - especially skiing, boxing and Pilates. My hobbies and work create in combination a perfect work-life-balance.
